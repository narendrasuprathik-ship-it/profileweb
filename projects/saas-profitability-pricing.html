SaaS Profitability Pricing

Manipal Institute of Technology • May 2024 - July 2024

Used regression and ensemble modeling across cohorts to identify profitable pricing interventions and reduce churn pressure.

  • Segmented SaaS businesses into three cohorts by subscriber scale.
  • Identified pricing metrics most associated with profitability outcomes.
  • Built decision framework for practical pricing adjustments.
  • Applied logistic regression and ensemble methods for profitability prediction.
  • Designed a 3C analytical pipeline to diagnose under-performance patterns.
  • Generated actionable forecasts under different pricing scenarios.
  • Observed approximately 43% ARR increase across modeled cohorts.
  • Estimated around 5% reduction in churn with adjusted plans.
  • Projected around 4% lift in customer lifetime value.
Decision quality improvement

Moving from static pricing to model-backed adjustments improved both retention and revenue confidence without overfitting to one segment.
